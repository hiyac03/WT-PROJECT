OUR MODELS

Brain Tumor Test

This classifies between 4 classes ie- meningioma, glioma and pituitary tumor and also normal scans. If a tumor is detected, the image will be passed through a segmentation model and the following output will be generated.

Retinal Test

This classifies between 4 classes ie- cataract, diabetic retinopathy and glaucoma and also normal retinal images. A feature to detect blood vessels in the eye fundus has also been implemented.

Upcoming

We are currently working on expanding the horizons of this detection system - integrating models based on lung diseases and various other conditions. Additionally, existing models are regularly trained and updated to regulate their performance and improve accuracy measures.

Is my medical data secure when using this project?

Data security and privacy are a top priority. All personal health information is protected following strict privacy regulations and standards to ensure your data remains confidential.

How does the brain tumor detection work?

The brain tumor detection utilizes medical imaging techniques such as MRI or CT scans. These scans are analyzed using advanced algorithms to identify and locate potential tumors.Efforts are made to continually improve accuracy through research and development.

What is a retinal test, and why is it important?

A retinal test involves capturing images of the retina at the back of the eye. It is crucial for early detection of various eye diseases, such as diabetic retinopathy or glaucoma, and can aid in preserving vision. Efforts are made to continually improve accuracy through research and development.

Is this project intended for medical professionals only?

No, this project is designed to benefit both medical professionals and individuals seeking regular health check-ups. It can assist doctors in diagnosis and provide users with valuable insights into their health.
